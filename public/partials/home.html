SmartHome is a home automation app. Features include the current temperature inside your home and right outside your house, monitors your open doors, includes alerts if you’ve left a door open, and looks to the weather forecast to advise you whether you need to water your lawn based on the upcoming weather forecast.

On the dashboard, users can see the temperature readings, status of their doors and any alerts.  Users can set a time limit for how long their door can be open before receiving an alert.  This is great for the forgetful person who leaves the garage door open.
